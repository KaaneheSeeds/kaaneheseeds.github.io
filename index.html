7 Fast-Growing Privacy Hedges for Hawaii

By Giovanni Kaanehe
Fast Growing

The best privacy hedges for Hawaii’s climate are fast-growing, towering, and clumping sedges. Sugarcane, bamboo, and heliconias are just a few to mention.

This article covers the top 7 fast-growing hedge options for your garden whether you live in Oahu, Big Island, Maui, Kauai etc. Read on to learn which ones grow best along your wall, what plants are good wind-blockers, which are good for providing shade, and what plants don't require maintenance.

7 Fast-Growing Privacy Hedges for Hawaii

Whether you are looking for plants that block wind or to filter the sunlight hitting the side of your house, these growing suggestions are your top picks for Hawaii's climate.

1. Sugarcane (Saccharum sp.)

Sugarcane has been growing in Hawaii for centuries. Luckily, this allows you to buy it in big quantities to fit any backyard project you desire.

Sugarcane grows very fast since it's a grass and handles harsh sun, making it perfect for privacy, wind, and sun blockers. And did I mention it's edible!

  • Other common names: Hawaiians refer to sugarcane as "kō"
  • Size at maturity: 10 to 20 feet.
  • Light Requirements: Full Sun
  • Soil Type: Well-draining and arieted
  • Tolerant of:Wind, harsh sun, dehydration, and salt spray.
  • Maintenance: - Water frequently but make sure soil dries completely before watering again - Wear gloves to pick up fallen leaves as sugarcane has fiberglass-like hairs similar to california grass.
